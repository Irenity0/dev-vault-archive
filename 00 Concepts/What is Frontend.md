**Frontend (Client-Side Development)** refers to the part of a website or web application that users interact with **directly**. It includes everything visible on the screen, such as **layouts, buttons, text, images, and animations**.

## **5Ô∏è‚É£ Frontend vs Backend**

|**Frontend**|**Backend**|
|---|---|
|Visible part of a website|Behind-the-scenes logic|
|Uses **HTML, CSS, JavaScript**|Uses **Node.js, Python, Java, PHP**|
|Runs in the **browser**|Runs on the **server**|
|Handles **UI/UX**|Handles **databases, APIs, authentication**|
